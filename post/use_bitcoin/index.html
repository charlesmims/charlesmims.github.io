Bitcoin is a form of currency that, like other currencies, is traded in open markets. Unlike other currencies, it’s based on something real (computer processor time and mathematical limits) and is actually a limited commodity. Also unlike other currencies, it’s very difficult to steal and can’t be faked. In my estimation, that makes it more valuable than the dollar. The only strength the dollar has over bitcoin is that it’s accepted in more places. The more people who use bitcoin, however, the more places are likely to accept it. So, if you feel like buying me a beer or whatever, set yourself up a bitcoin wallet (if you don’t have one already) and send me a little!

Here are a some links to help you get started. - Manage your wallet at https://blockchain.info/wallet/#/ - Buy bitcoin at https://www.coinbase.com/ - Track the market at https://bitcoinwisdom.com/
