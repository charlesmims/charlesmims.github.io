blacklisting mbeans from Cassandra in jxm_exporter

Wed Sep 13, 2017 by Charles Mims

Why: There is a bug in Cassandra v3.0.14 that was preventing cleanup of sstables while tombstones were being polled. https://issues.apache.org/jira/browse/CASSANDRA-13752 As I understand it, this is happening because the method that cleans them up and the method that reports the metrics step on eachother’s feet – getting metrics on tombstones interrups the cleanup and corrupts the table. It’s supposed to be fixed in 3.0.15 due next week, but disabling the polling of this mbean should stop the badness. Read more →
